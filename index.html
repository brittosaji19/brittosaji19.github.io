M.P. Joseph is from the 1978 batch of the Indian Administrative Service (IAS) Kerala Cadre. Earlier, he had been selected to the 1977 batch of the Indian Police Service (IPS), allotted to Kerala Cadre. He joined the Indian Police Service and completed his training at the Sardar Vallabhai Patel National Police Academy, Hyderabad. Having worked earlier as a Probationary Officer in the State Bank of India, he also comes with a strong financial and banking background. Joseph holds a Masters Degree in Human Resources Development from the Victoria University of Manchester, UK and a Masters Degree in Solid State Physics from the Cochin University of Science and Technology. He is an aluminus of the prestigious Loyola College, Chennai.


M. P. Joseph is presently Advisor to Government (Labour Reforms, Industrial Relations), in the Rank of Additional Chief Secretary, Government of Kerala. M. P. Joseph is also the Executive Vice Chairman of Bhavanam Foundation Kerala a new public sector non-profit Company fully owned by the Government of Kerala and incorporated on 11 Jan 14 with the objective of providing own houses and flats to Labourers, low paid employees in the government, public and private sectors.

Joseph had an illustrious career in the IAS Kerala Cadre starting his career in the state as Sub Collector Trichur and later moving on as District Collector, Ernakulm, where he also concurrently held charge as Mayor of the Corporation of Cochin for nearly 2 Â½ years.. Later he was Labour Commissioner, Kerala, for the longest ever period for any Labour Commissioner in the state.
